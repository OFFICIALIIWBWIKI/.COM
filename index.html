The Reactor

Category: Important glowing machinery • Status: dramatic • Danger level: yes

The reactor is the beating, blinking, overreacting heart of the IIWB experience. It does not simply exist. It performs. Sometimes it glows like a proud science project, and sometimes it behaves like it just read terrible news.

"The reactor is perfectly safe," said somebody, moments before it started making a very concerning noise.

According to highly reliable hallway rumors, the reactor can sense three things: nearby chaos, bad engineering decisions, and people who say "what does this button do?" with too much confidence.

Ollie

Category: Character • Known from: Classic Mines

Ollie is the kind of character who appears in Classic Mines and instantly gives the impression that he knows exactly where all the trouble is hidden. Whether he is exploring, surviving, or just standing there like a legend, he brings classic energy to the scene.

His public image can best be described as: "calm on the outside, probably hearing cave noises on the inside." Fans believe he is either extremely brave or simply too used to the weirdness.

Article: Albert

Albert

Category: Character • Known from: Sea Journey • Side business: Bloxy Cola distribution

Albert is the iconic Bloxy Cola seller from Sea Journey and the brother of Ollie. While some characters seek adventure, Albert seeks customers, stock efficiency, and maximum cola-based happiness.

He has the energy of a man who could sell a cold drink during a storm and somehow make it sound like a premium lifestyle upgrade. If you hear a cheerful sales pitch near the sea, there is a strong chance Albert is nearby.

"One Bloxy Cola for you, one Bloxy Cola for the journey, and one emergency Bloxy Cola just in case." - Albert, definitely

Many believe Albert's true superpower is running a refreshment business in locations where the weather, the map, and common sense all try to say no.
